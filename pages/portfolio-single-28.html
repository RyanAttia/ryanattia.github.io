Bellabeat Case Study

Project Description

Conducted an end-to-end data analysis case study to help Bellabeat, a wellness technology company, optimize marketing strategies for their health-focused smart devices. Leveraged publicly available Fitbit fitness tracker data from 30 users, performing thorough data cleaning, normalization, and merging using Python (Pandas) and Jupyter Notebooks.

Executed exploratory data analysis (EDA) with Matplotlib and Seaborn to identify key user behavior trends, such as activity patterns correlating with calories burned, weekday versus weekend variations, and the impact of sleep duration on physical activity. Created compelling visualizations including heatmaps, distribution plots, and bar charts to communicate insights to executive and marketing teams. Based on the analysis, recommended actionable marketing strategies such as targeted campaigns around active hours, inactivity push notifications, and workweek-focused engagement initiatives.

Project Date May 2025
